Why Choose SGU?

SAM Global University, established in 2019 under the umbrella of the Guru Hargobind Society, has emerged as the best private university in Madhya Pradesh, India. With a rich legacy of six decades, SGU has been imparting quality education and setting new benchmarks in the field. The university holds the approval of the Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidhyalaya (Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Adhiniyam 2007 and UGC, further validating its commitment to excellence.

We also balance legacy with innovation and are on an incessant journey of evolution. We are the first AI-enabled University of Central India to show our commitment towards innovation, technology, and progress for students.

AI Modules That Augment Every Interaction

From personalised mentoring to simulation labs and research copilots — our AI modules augment every classroom, lab and counselling interaction.

01

SAHA

Academic Counselor

AI-driven counseling optimizes traditional paradigms through perpetual accessibility, individualized interventions, and superior operational scalability.

02

BODHI

AI Tutor

AI tutoring interfaces optimize smart classroom content delivery by facilitating adaptive, immersive, and individualized learning experiences.

03

AVYUKTAA

Simulation Lab

A fully implemented simulation lab providing students with access to hands-on learning experiences, allowing them to conduct experiments and simulations in a virtual environment.

04

ANVII

Research Assistant

An AI research assistant who helps improve research processes and project evaluations through intelligent insights and automation.

05

AAJA

3D Hologram Presenter

A 3D hologram that provides interactive demonstrations where participants can interact with the speaker more engagingly in virtual events and workshops.

06

BAANI

AI Evaluator

An evaluator bot that assists with periodical and continuous campus evaluations with speed and accuracy.

07

PADMAA

AI Interview Coach

Helps students better prepare for internships and job interviews with AI-driven mock interviews, feedback, and coaching.

08

E-BLOCK

Credential Verification

Offers enhanced security and transparency for academic records through decentralized access and lifelong digital verification.

09

IRAAJ

Humanoid Robots

Humanoid robots capable of making complex decisions based on environmental cues, bringing AI into everyday campus interactions.
